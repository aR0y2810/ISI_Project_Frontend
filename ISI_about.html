About Us

Our Founders

Our hotel has its roots with the king of the region, who set up a guest house beside his royal palace to accomodate foreign visitors. Over the centuries, the guest house has been renovated and passed hands many times until we reach its present iteration, an epitome of luxury in the heart of the city.

Our Mission

At Hotel Imperial International, our mission is to provide an exceptional hospitality experience that exceeds the expectations of every guest. We are committed to offering the finest in luxury, comfort, and personalized service, ensuring a memorable stay for leisure and business travelers alike. Through our elegant accommodations, world-class dining, and state-of-the-art amenities, we create a welcoming atmosphere where guests feel valued and cared for. Sustainability and community engagement are also at the heart of our mission, as we strive to make a positive impact while delivering unparalleled service in every aspect of the guest experience.
